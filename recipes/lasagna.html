Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked dish made by stacking wide, flat pasta sheets with alternating layers of savory sauce (like ragù), rich cheeses (like ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan), and often meat or vegetables.

Ingredients

    The Pasta:

  • 12 lasagna noodles

    • The meat sauce:

  • ½ lb ground Italian sausage
  • 1 medium yellow onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 cups (approx. 24 oz) marinara or pasta sauce
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
